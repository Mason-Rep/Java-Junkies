Employee services:

Employee login - The employee needs a username attached to thier first name and last name, needs a password, address, phone number, and email.
Employee reimburstment request - The employee can make a reimbursement request. The employee needs itemized reciept. The accepted catagories are gas, lodging, food, and services to stay conecceted.
Employee can view reimburstment request history - This lets the employee view the current pending reimburstment and finalized reimburstments. The finalized show which parts were award reimburstments and which were denied.
Employee can view their information and update thier information - The employee can see and update first name, last name, password, address, phone number, and email. They can only view job title, salary, and PTO.
Employee recives email - when reimburstment is resolved employee gets email about the request.
